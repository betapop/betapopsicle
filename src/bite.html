"Why so serious? Let me pour you some juice."

Vampire Cookie is a rare/epic cookie in the Cookie Run franchise. He's a very laid-back individual who tends to go with the flow. He also has a little sister named Alchemist Cookie who he cares for deeply. Contrary to his sister, he would rather laze and relax then work or create.

 

fun things...

I am obsessed with this guy. No specific reason either, minus a little bit of nostalgia for when I first got into the franchise. I do really like his design and his silly little grin, though. I feel very lucky that he's an older cookie since he has a lot more content spread out over games! I really love his relationship with the other juice bar members and his sister as well. He loves his friends and family... Anyways, shout out to my beloved
